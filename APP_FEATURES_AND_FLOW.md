## 1. üè† Home Screen (The Task List)
The first screen you see. It‚Äôs where you plan your day.

*   **Daily Task List:** Shows all your tasks for the selected date.
*   **Date Navigator:**
    *   A horizontal calendar strip at the top.
    *   You can swipe left/right to change days.
    *   **"Today" Button:** Quickly jumps back to the current date.
*   **Progress Ring:** A visual circle showing how much of your day is completed (based on task duration).

## 2. ‚ö° Live Focus Mode (Landscape View)
The heart of the app. This is the "Timeline" view when you rotate your phone sideways.

*   **The Timeline:**
    *   Think of it like a calendar or a video editor timeline.
    *   Time flows from left to right (e.g., 8:00 AM -> 9:00 AM -> 10:00 AM).
*   **The "NOW" Line:**
    *   A glowing **Cyan Laser Line** that cuts vertically through the screen.
    *   It stays in the specific center or moves with time to show *exactly* where you are in your day.
*   **"Swimlanes":**
    *   Horizontal rows for different tasks (like lanes in a swimming pool).
    *   Allows you to see overlapping tasks or parallel activities.
*   **Glass Task Blocks:**
    *   Your tasks appear as glass rectangles on the timeline.
    *   **Drag & Drop:** You can tap and drag a block to reschedule it.
    *   **Pinch to Resize:** You can make a task longer or shorter by pinching it (just like zooming into a photo).
*   **The Dock (Bottom Bar):**
    *   **Play Button:** Starts the timer for the selected task.
    *   **Quick Stats:** Small icons showing your current streak or total focus time.

## 3. ‚è≥ Focus Timer (Active Mode)
When you hit "Play" on a task, you enter this immersive mode.

*   **Full-Screen Timer:**
    *   Distraction-free interface.
    *   Giant numbers (e.g., "45:00") showing time remaining.
    *   **Background:** Deep dark mode with a subtle pulse effect.
*   **Progress Bar:**
    *   A massive, screen-filling bar that slowly fills up as time passes.
    *   Gives you a peripheral sense of time without needing to read numbers.
*   **Slide to Complete:**
    *   Instead of a boring "Stop" button, you **slide a glowing handle** across the bottom to finish the session. It feels satisfying, like unlocking a phone.
*   **Audio Focus:**
    *   Plays optional white noise or focus sounds (Rain, Cafe, etc.) during the timer.

*   **Leave Management:**
    *   Mark specific dates as "Leave" or "Holiday".
    *   The app won't schedule recurring tasks on these days.

    *   Ideally keeps count of how many times in a row you've done a recurring habit.

### D. üîî Notifications & Sounds
